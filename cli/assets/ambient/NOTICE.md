# Cliks Embedded Room Tones

These optional personal room tones are bundled in the Cliks binary, normalized
for quiet background listening, and never sent to a team or backend.

All six source works are licensed CC0:

- `rain.mp3`: "Rain on Window Loop" by alxl
  - https://opengameart.org/content/rain-on-window-loop
- `fire.mp3`: "Fireplace Sound Loop" by PagDev
  - https://opengameart.org/content/fireplace-sound-loop
- `cafe.mp3`: "Coffee House Bump" by OatCog
  - https://opengameart.org/content/coffee-house-bump
- `cloud.mp3`: "Heavenly Loop" by isaiah658
  - https://opengameart.org/content/heavenly-loop
- `contemplation.mp3`: "Contemplation" by Joth
  - https://opengameart.org/content/contemplation-0
- `downtempo.mp3`: "Music loop strong downtempo seamless" by Nostromo
  - https://opengameart.org/content/music-loop-strong-downtempo-seamless

The distributed MP3 files are transcoded from the listed source downloads at
44.1 kHz stereo and normalized to a restrained listening level. CC0 does not
require attribution, but Cliks keeps this notice so the origin stays clear.
